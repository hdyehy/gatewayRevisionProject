Barcelona

Barcelona is a coastal city in the heart of Spain! with a rich, cultured history, it's no wonder why it's frequently referred to as the place to visit in Spain.

Welcome to Beautiful Barcelona!

Spain's largest port and second largest city, it is the country's main industrial and commercial centre, as well as a major cultural and educational source. Whether you come here for the football, the stunning architecture, sandy beaches, or vibrant festivals, you will surely find a place here in the city of counts.

football

The home town of FC Barcelona. FC Barcelona is renowned for its historically skillful and attractive brand of attacking football that places an emphasis on flowing, open play. One of the most famous football clubs in the entire league, fan loyalty and enthusiasm is palpaple in the Barcelona air!

Storied History

With a rich history spanning millenia, Barcelona The settlement history of Barcelona starts in the 2nd century BC with Barcino, the largest known Roman village outside Rome. But the Gothic and the time of Modernism have left their traces still visible today as well. The history of Barcelona is also characterized by the quest for independence and maintaining their own cultural identity.

unique architecture

The city first made its mark at the Barcelona World's fair in 1888 when its greatest emerging architects, Gaudi, Casanovas, and Montaner introduced Catalan modernism or Modernisme to the world. At the time, none of Gaudi's major works had been completed and La Sagrada Familia had only just started construction. Now, Barcelona is host to some of the most unique and varied architecture the world knows.
