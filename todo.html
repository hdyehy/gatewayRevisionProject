Activities

Barcelona has seemingly endless amount of things to discover and adventures to behold! If youre not sure where to start, we made a list of our favorites for you!

Our Top Picks

FC Barcelona Games

Enjoy the thrilling highs and devestating lows of European football! The infamous soccer club Futbol Club Barcelona, call this city their home. Winners of a record-breaking 75 trophies, love for FCB in Barcelona is in high supply! expect lively games with roaring fans, tasty concessions, all you can drink beer tickets, and an unforgettable sports experience for the whole family!

Location: Camp Nou Stadium, Barcelona

Price: €89-150 EUR

Target Age: All ages!

Barceloneta Beach

Located near the stunning gothic quarter and in the fishing quarter of the same name, Barceloneta is by far the most famous beach in all of Spain! Enjoy a sunny day tanning on the golden sand beach, swim in the beautiful warm, blue, and gentle water, or test your skills in a friendly game of volleyball! Enjoy peace of mind when the kids are swimming with plentiful lifeguards and local police. feel free to use any of the free sun loungers, changing rooms and restrooms, umbrellas, and even wifi! Grab a tasty bite to eat and enjoy a tasty seafood dish or the world famous "tapas", a wide variety of small appetizer like dishes!

Location: La Barceloneta, Barcelona

Price: Free, food available for purchase

Target Age: All Ages!

Museu Nacional d'Art de Catalunya

National Art Museum of Catalonia, or known as MNAC, is one of the largest and most prestigious museums in all of Spain. Host to over a staggering 290 thousand pieces of art, MNAC is best known for it's outstanding collection of rare ancient Roman church paintings and murals! With over a thousands years of art to enjoy, it's hard not to want spend your entire trip in this one museum! Outside of the romanesque murals exhibit, the museum also includes many others such as modernism, gothic art, great European Renaissance painters, modern photography, and a large exhibit for kids to get their hands on the action!

Location:Parc de Montjuïc, Barcelona

Price: €2-15 EUR

Target Age: Nine and older

Montserrat Natural Park

Fancy some scenery outside of the bustling Barcelona city life? Then you don't want to miss the Montserrat Natural Park! Located just an hour outside of Barcelona, this remarkable park is home to the best nature in all of Spain! With miles of nature and hiking trails you can walk, theres also ziplining adventures, fauna site seeing, and guided hiking tours. Learn about the peculiarly shaped mountains and their suprising religous history.

Location: Montserrat Natural Park

Price: Free

Target Age: 12 to 65

La Sagrida Familia

The unfinished magnum opus of architect visionary, Antoni Gaudi, first began construction all the way back in 1882 with construction slated to finally finish in 2026. While Gaudi's work litters Barcelona and Spain (and we highly recommend discovering his other works on your visit!), La Sagrida Familia is truly a sight to behold. Grab a bite in the plaza and take in the sights of the chapel in all it's stunning glory or get up close and personal with a paid tour of the jaw dropping interior!

Location: Mallorca, Barcelona

Price:€26-30 EUR

Target Age: All Ages!

El Raval District

located in the heart of the city's Ciutat Vella, the old quarters, the El Raval district is one the most unique and fun districts in all of Barcelona! A colorful, vibrant neighborhood with an interesting history, there is so much to do and discover around every corner. Enjoy cutting edge exhibitions of contemporary art, find hidden gems at unique thrift and vintage shops, have a night out with friends at one of the many bustling bars and night clubs, or simply enjoy the many street performers and artists.

Location:El Raval, Barcelona

Free to visit, with various activities and shops

Target Age: Young Adults
